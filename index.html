Renewable Energy Sources

Renewable energy technologies use naturally replenished resources to help support modern energy systems.

☀ Solar Energy

Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity and are widely used in homes, commercial buildings, and large-scale solar farms.

🌬 Wind Energy

Wind turbines generate electricity using natural wind movement and continue to grow globally.

💧 Hydropower

Hydropower systems use flowing water to drive turbines and produce renewable electricity.

🌋 Geothermal

Geothermal systems use heat beneath the Earth’s surface for electricity and heating applications.

Good Effects of Renewable Energy

Renewable energy technologies contribute to cleaner and more diversified energy systems.

⚡ Sustainable Power

🌍 Energy Diversification
